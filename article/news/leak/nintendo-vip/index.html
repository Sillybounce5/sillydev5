Nintendo VIP Membership leak

By Silly 29-07-2022

As Nintendo's quietest year slowly passes a buddy of mine leaks a language document for a page on their website.

Earlier this morning, Jim sent me, and some others, the .htlang file for a page on their website titled "VIP Membership"

The file states that this new VIP Membership will contain the following:

  • Nintendo Online + Expansion
  • Free Monthly Games
  • The ability to trial any game
  • Discounts on all products on the My Nintendo Store
