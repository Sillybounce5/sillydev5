Set-up

If you bought a new SD card you'll have to format it to FAT32 using one of the links above, and then put it in your 3ds and take a photo, else,
You can just unplug the SD Card and put it in your PC/Phone

Once you have the SD Card in your PC/Phone, go inside the "Nintendo 3DS" Folder, there should be one folder with a string of random characters as the name, copy down these numbers, you'll need them for later.

Step 1: The Files

Download and unzip boot9strap.zip to your PC/Phone

Copy all files inside the folder to the root of your SD card

Step 2: FRIENDS!!!

Insert the SD card back into your 3DS, and open the Friends List app, and copy down your friend code

Go to the Bruteforce Movable website

In the friend code section type in your friends code, and in the ID section type in the string ofcharacters we copied down earlier.
Press "Go", and after a while a friend code will appear on the page, go to your 3DS friends app (keep the page open on your PC/Phone) and add that friend code,
Back on the page just wait for the proccess to reach step 4, and then download the movable.sed

Step 3: Thats unSAFE!

On your PC/Phone go to the unSAFE_MODE website

On this website upload the file you downloaded in the last step, and then press the "Build & Download" Button. You will download a zip file, extract this file.
Move the usm.bin file to the root of the SD card, then go into the 'Nintendo 3DS' Folder, and go into the special characters folder, inside that folder you will see another folder with a similar name, go inside that folder and create a new folder called "Nintendo DSiWare".
Copy the F00D43D5.bin into the new folder. Now eject the sd card and put it back in the 3DS and reboot the console.

Step 4: Installation

On the 3DS open the system settings app and go to data management, now tap DSiWare and select the MicroSD tab,
This will take you to this intimidating-looking bannerbomb menu, Press A, and the system will turn off.

Step 5: Finger Twister

Now that the system is off, Hold down L + R + D-pad up + A + Power for 2 seconds, this will enable safe mode
On this screen follow the instructions to update your 3DS console, WHEN the error screen pops up press ok, press yes to opening internet settings,
Now click connection 1, and change settings, now go across to the next menu on the left and press proxy settings, then detailed setup.
When the option appears press A to reboot.

Step 6: Luma

On the Luma3DS menu select 'Show NAND or user string in system settings', then press START.

On the system settings app, go to data management, now tap DSiWare and select the MicroSD tab, on the menu select restore slots.

Power the DS off then put the SD card into your PC/Phone, and delete the F00D43D5.bin file you placed on the SD card earlier

Step 7: Homebrew Menu

Insert the card back into the 3DS and open the download play app, when the app opens press L + D-Pad Down + SELECT to open the Rosalina menu, scroll down to misc. options, and press A on the first choice
Keep pressing B until the rosalina menu closes, and then close the download play app. Open download play again, and on the homebrew launcher click FBI (This is the software that installs .CIA files), click SD, then cias, select current directory,then select install all CIAs.
Close download play, and unwrap the present boxes. Now power off the system.

Step 8: I AM GOD!

Press and hold START + Power for 1 second, this opens the godmode menu, go through the setup, and when the main menu appears press HOME, select Scripts, then GM9 MEGASCRIPT, select 'Scripts from plailect's guide' then select setup luma3ds to CTRNAND, when done select cleanup SD card. Then keep pressing B and exit godmode.
