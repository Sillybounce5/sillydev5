Step 1: Allowing sideloading

FireTV OS (fOS) is pretty simple to homebrew, since it is based on android, which accepts sideloading using .apk files.
But on fOS you need to enable this in settings.

But first, you'll need to download an app from the appstore titled 'Downloader' and is exactly what it says on the tin, now enable developer options.

To do this go to the Home screen and click the settings icon, go to the My Fire TV menu, then the about menu. Click the top option 7 times, and now you should be a developer

In the new developer options menu click 'ADB Debugging' so it's on and also click'Install unknown apps' and then click on Downloader
